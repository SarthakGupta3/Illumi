How do I play the bedtime stories, ambient sounds, and classic lullabies?

All of the audio files are located on our micro USB drive that is included with your purchase. Simply plug the USB drive into the back side of the star projector and enjoy.

Can I play the bedtime stories, ambient sounds, and classic lullabies in the car?

Yes, simply plug our micro USB drive into your vehicles USB port and your children can listen to all of our bedtime stories, ambient sounds, and classic lullabies while you drive.
